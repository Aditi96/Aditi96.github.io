Aditi Mishra

I am a Computer Science PhD student at School of Computing and Augmented Intelligence, Arizona State University, where I work with Dr. Chris Bryan on data visualization.

My interests particularly lie in designing and developing interactive interfaces to enable autonomous agent interpretability and explanability for novice end users. I am also interested in applying machine learning to reconstruct large unstructured scientific datasets.

I am currently in the job market for HCI Research Scientist or Postdoctoral roles!!
